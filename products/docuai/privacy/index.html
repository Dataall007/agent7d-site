DocuAI for monday.com

Privacy Policy

DocuAI for monday.com · Last updated: July 1, 2026
The short version: DocuAI reads your monday.com item data to generate documents. We do not store your data after the document is created. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

DocuAI ("we", "our", "us") is a monday.com Marketplace application developed and operated by agent7d.com, based in Israel. For questions, contact us at support@agent7d.com.

2. What Data We Access

When you use DocuAI, we access the following data from your monday.com account:

  • Item data from the board where DocuAI is installed (column values, item name, status, etc.)
  • Board structure (column names and types) — to detect board type and relevant fields
  • User profile (name, email) — to personalize documents
  • Files column — to attach generated documents back to your item

We access this data only at the moment of document generation, and only for the specific item you are working on.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your monday.com item data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate the document you requested (via AI)
  • Attach the generated document to your monday.com item's Files column
  • Display document generation history within the app (stored locally in monday.com App Storage, accessible only to your account)

4. Data Retention

We do not store your business data. Item data (deal values, contact names, notes, etc.) is processed in real-time to generate the document and is not saved to any DocuAI database or server after generation is complete.

The only data we retain:

  • Document generation history (document type, date, item name) — stored in monday.com App Storage, not on our servers
  • App settings (language preference, company name for document headers) — stored in monday.com App Storage

5. AI Processing (Claude by Anthropic)

DocuAI uses Claude, an AI model developed by Anthropic, to generate document content. Your item data is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing: anthropic.com/privacy.

Anthropic does not use API data to train its models.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Anthropic — for AI document generation (see Section 5)
  • monday.com — as required to operate within the monday.com platform
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or legal process

7. Security

All data transmitted between DocuAI, monday.com, and Anthropic is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. We follow security best practices to protect your data during processing.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (document history in App Storage)
  • Delete your document history at any time from the DocuAI History tab
  • Uninstall DocuAI at any time — this removes all app data from your account
  • Request data deletion by contacting us at support@agent7d.com

9. GDPR & International Users

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (providing the service you requested).

10. Children's Privacy

DocuAI is a business application and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of DocuAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
