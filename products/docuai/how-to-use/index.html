Welcome to DocuAI! 🎉
DocuAI generates professional documents directly from your monday.com item data — using AI. No copy-paste, no manual typing. Just click Generate and get a ready-to-send PDF and Word document in seconds.
Getting Started

How to use DocuAI

1
Open any item in monday.com
Click on any item in your board to open the item view. DocuAI works from the item panel — look for the DocuAI tab at the top of the item.
2
DocuAI detects your board automatically
DocuAI reads your board type (Leads, Deals, Contacts, or any board) and shows the most relevant document templates first. No setup or mapping required.
💡 You'll see "All Boards", "Leads Board", or "Deals Board" in the top right of the panel
3
Choose a document type
Select from 6 document types. Click Generate → on the template you want. Some templates may ask for a few additional details (like services or payment terms).
4
Wait ~15 seconds while AI writes your document
You'll see a 3-step progress bar: Reading your data → Writing with AI → Finishing touches. The AI reads your item data and writes a complete, professional document — not just fills in blanks.
5
Download and share
When ready, you'll see a preview of the AI-written content, and two download buttons: PDF and Word (.docx). The document is also automatically attached to your item's Files tab.
✅ "Attached to Files" confirmation appears on the success screen

Pro Tips

Get better AI output

📋
Fill your item columns: The more data in your item (Deal Value, Notes, Status, Contact info), the smarter and more specific the AI output will be.
📝
Use the Updates/Notes column: Adding meeting notes or deal context in your item's Updates section significantly improves Meeting Summary and Lead Brief quality.
🔍
Use Deep mode for client-facing documents: For proposals, contracts, and ROI reports you'll send to clients, Deep mode produces noticeably richer content.
FAQ

Common questions

Why does generation take 13–20 seconds?
DocuAI uses AI to write your document from scratch — not just fill in a template. The AI reads all your item data, understands the context, and writes complete professional content. This takes a bit longer than simple templates, but the quality is significantly higher.
Where is the generated document saved?
Every document is automatically attached to your monday.com item's Files tab. You can also download PDF and Word versions directly from the DocuAI success screen.
I don't see all 6 templates. Where are they?
DocuAI shows the most relevant templates for your board type first. Use the "Show all templates" toggle at the bottom of the panel to see all 6 templates on any board.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your item data is used only to generate the document and is not stored on DocuAI servers after generation. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I generate in Hebrew?
Go to the Settings tab in the DocuAI panel and switch Language to Hebrew (עברית). All documents will be generated in Hebrew with RTL layout.
