Frequently Asked Questions

How long does document generation take?
Generation takes 13–20 seconds depending on the mode selected. Fast ⚡ mode is quickest (~13s), Deep 🔍 mode produces richer content but takes ~20s. This is normal — the AI is writing a full document, not just filling in fields.
Why doesn't DocuAI show all templates on my board?
DocuAI automatically detects your board type and shows the most relevant templates first. For example, on a Leads board, Lead Brief appears as the primary template. Use the "Show all templates" toggle to see all 6 templates on any board.
How do I generate documents in Hebrew?
Go to the Settings tab in the DocuAI panel and switch the language to Hebrew (עברית). All subsequent documents will be generated in Hebrew with full right-to-left (RTL) support.
Where is the generated document saved?
Generated documents are automatically attached to your monday.com item's Files tab. You can also download both PDF and Word (.docx) versions directly from the DocuAI success screen.
Is my data safe? Does DocuAI store my business data?
No. Your item data is processed in real-time to generate the document and is not stored on DocuAI's servers. Only document history metadata (type, date, item name) is saved in monday.com App Storage — not your actual business data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The document content looks too generic. How can I improve it?
The more data in your monday.com item, the better the AI output. Make sure your item has filled columns (Deal Value, Notes/Updates, Status, Contact info). Using the "Deep 🔍" generation mode also produces richer, more specific content.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your DocuAI subscription at any time from monday.com's App Marketplace → My Apps → DocuAI → Manage Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
